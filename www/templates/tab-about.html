Centaurus Stellar Wallet
Stellar aims for the stars. And the nearest star system to us is Alpha Centauri! The next step to get there is a functional app for sending and receiving payments (starting with STR, only).
Features
  • Display balance (STR)
  • Send by manually pasting an adress
  • Send by scanning a QR-code
  • Generate a QR-code for receiving
  • Coming soon: Display recent transactions
    •
    Getting Started
  • Go to the receive tab and share your address with a friend who already has Stellars.
  • If you have a facebook account, you're lucky. Go to launch.stellar.org, create an account. Authenticate with Facebook and get Stellars for free, worth about 10$.
  • Browse stellartalk.org and find more alternatives
    •
    Known Issues
    First of all you should be aware, that this app is in a very early stage and errors are still likely to occur. Don't hold more Stellars in this app than you need for every day purpose and you can afford to loose in worst case.

  • There is currently no backup functionality. If you lose your phone, uninstall the app, or clear the app data, your remaining Stellars are lost forever!
  • Connectivity may fail for multiple reasons. In this case you cannot do anything with the app. Your address remains displayed as "loading".
  • Stellar Names are not supported.
  • You cannot use an existing address. It's always created on first use
