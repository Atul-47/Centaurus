Centaurus Stellar Wallet (1.0)
Stellar aims for the stars. And the nearest star system to us is Alpha Centauri! The next step to get there is a functional app for sending and receiving payments.
Features
  • Display balance
  • Send by manually pasting an adress
  • Send by scanning a QR-code
  • Cross currency payments
  • Generate a QR-code for receiving
  • Display recent transactions
  • Encrypted backups
    •
    Getting Started
  • Use the generated address or create a stellar account at launch.stellar.org and import your keys from there.
  • Go to the receive tab and share your address with a friend who already has Stellars.
  • If you have a facebook account, you're lucky! On launch.stellar.org, authenticate with facebook and get Stellars for free, worth about 10$.
  • Browse stellartalk.org and find more alternatives
  • Make a backup of your wallet and keep it safe! Otherwise, if you lose your phone, uninstall the app, or clear the app data, your remaining Stellars (and all future payments to this address) are lost forever!

    Known Issues
    First of all you should be aware, that this app is in a very early stage and errors are still likely to occur. Don't hold more Stellars in this app than you need for every day purpose and you can afford to loose in worst case.

  • Connectivity may fail for multiple reasons. In this case you cannot do anything with the app. Your address remains displayed as "loading".
  • Stellar Names are not supported.
  • Account reserve is not calculated correctly.
