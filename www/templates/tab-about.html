Stellar aims for the stars. And the nearest star system to us is Alpha Centauri! The next step to get there is a functional app for sending and receiving payments (starting with STR, only)

  • Login to an existing Stellar account
  • Display balance (STR)
  • Send by manually pasting an adress
  • Send by scanning a QR-code
  • Generate a QR-code for receiving
  • Display recent transactions
  • Lock sending by local password
